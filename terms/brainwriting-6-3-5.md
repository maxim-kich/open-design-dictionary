# Brainwriting 6-3-5

## Definition
Six people write 3 ideas in 5 minutes then rotate papers.

## Examples
- 108 ideas in 30 minutes
- Silent generation phase
- Build on others' ideas
- Structured rotation

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Strict time limits
- Silent writing
- Read before adding
- Full 6 rounds

**Don'ts**
- Verbal discussion
- Skip rotations
- Ignore previous ideas
- Fewer than 6 people
