# Churn Rate

## Definition
Percentage users abandoning product over time period.

## Examples
- Monthly churn 4.2%
- Revenue churn 3.1%
- 90-day churn analysis
- Segment churn by cohort

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Track revenue churn
- Cohort churn curves
- Exit survey correlation
- Win-back experiments

**Don'ts**
- Gross churn only
- No revenue analysis
- Monthly aggregate only
- Ignore reactivation
