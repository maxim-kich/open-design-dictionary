# Content Facet

## Definition
Attribute used to filter content collections.

## Examples
- Product: Color, Size, Material
- Article: Author, Date, Tag
- Jobs: Location, Role, Experience
- Events: Type, Date, Location

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- 3-7 facets maximum
- Clear business value
- Multiple selection
- Descriptive labels

**Don'ts**
- Irrelevant attributes
- More than 10 facets
- Single select only
- Internal metadata
