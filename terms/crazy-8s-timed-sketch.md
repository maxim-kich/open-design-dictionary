# Crazy 8s Timed Sketch

## Definition
Fold paper into 8 sections sketch one idea per minute.

## Examples
- 8 homepage variations
- 8 checkout flows
- 8 onboarding screens
- 8-minute total exercise

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Strict one minute
- Rough sketches ok
- Different each panel
- Share and discuss

**Don'ts**
- Detailed drawings
- Repeat same idea
- Skip panels
- No time pressure
