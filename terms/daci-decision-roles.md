# DACI Decision Roles

## Definition
Driver Approver Contributor Informed responsibilities.

## Examples
- Product manager = Driver
- VP Product = Approver
- Design Eng = Contributors
- Marketing = Informed

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Clear single Driver
- Approver has authority
- Contributors have voice
- Document decisions

**Don'ts**
- Multiple Drivers
- No clear Approver
- Silent Contributors
- All Informed
