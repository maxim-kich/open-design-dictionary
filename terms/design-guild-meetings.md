# Design Guild Meetings

## Definition
Cross-team community of practice sharing.

## Examples
- Component library updates
- Design system metrics
- Cross-team critique
- Tooling workflow sharing

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Rotate facilitators
- Actionable takeaways
- Guest speakers
- Published notes

**Don'ts**
- Design-only attendees
- Status updates only
- No cross-pollination
- Secret discussions
