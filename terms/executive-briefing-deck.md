# Executive Briefing Deck

## Definition
Concise 5-8 slide leadership communication.

## Examples
- Problem 1 slide
- Solution 2 slides
- Proof 1 slide
- Ask 1 slide

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- 5-8 slides maximum
- Data visualization
- Clear single ask
- Rehearsed 10min delivery

**Don'ts**
- 25+ slide decks
- Text walls
- Multiple competing asks
- Unprepared delivery
