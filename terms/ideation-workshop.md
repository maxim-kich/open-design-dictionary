# Ideation Workshop

## Definition
Structured creative problem solving session.

## Examples
- Crazy 8s + dot voting
- 2hr cross-functional
- 50+ ideas generated
- Top 3 prioritized

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Diverse participants
- Structured methods
- Quantity over quality
- Clear prioritization

**Don'ts**
- Homogeneous group
- Open-ended discussion
- Criticism during generation
- No decision method
