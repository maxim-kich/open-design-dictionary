# OKR Framework

## Definition
Objectives (inspirational) Key Results (measurable).

## Examples
- Q2 Objective: Double MAU
- KR1: D30 retention 28%
- 3-5 KRs per objective
- Quarterly cadence

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Ambitious stretch goals
- 3-5 measurable KRs
- Cross-team visibility
- Weekly OKR checkins

**Don'ts**
- Too many OKRs
- Vague qualitative KRs
- Annual planning cycle
- Secret team OKRs
