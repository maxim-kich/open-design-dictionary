# Onboarding Process

## Definition
New hire first 90 days structured experience.

## Examples
- Day 1: Welcome orientation
- Week 1: Team intros training
- Month 1: First project
- 90-day check-in

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Buddy mentorship program
- Clear milestones
- Feedback checkpoints
- Equipment Day 0

**Don'ts**
- Sink or swim
- No clear milestones
- Single orientation day
- No feedback loop
