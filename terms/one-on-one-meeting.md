# One-on-One Meeting

## Definition
Regular manager-direct report sync cadence.

## Examples
- 30min weekly
- Employee agenda first
- Career growth focus
- Action items documented

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- Employee owns agenda
- 80% listening
- Follow-through tracking
- Rotate meeting formats

**Don'ts**
- Status reporting
- Manager monologue
- No agenda preparation
- Irregular cadence
