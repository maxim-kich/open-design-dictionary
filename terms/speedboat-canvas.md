# Speedboat Canvas

## Definition
Visual metaphor identifying what slows team or product progress.

## Examples
- Anchors as blockers
- Wind as accelerators
- Rocks as risks
- Destination as goal

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do's**
- All team participates
- Prioritize anchors
- Action items from blockers
- Regular revisits

**Don'ts**
- Blame individuals
- Ignore small anchors
- Skip action planning
- One-time exercise only
